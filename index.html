About Malum Coin (MLT)

Blockchain Revolution

Malum is Building blockchain, e-commerce, Defi, Metaverse and NFT products to derive new kinds of value from crypto technology.
MALUM is a Blockchain-based project that yearns to make people all across the world use cryptocurrencies to lead their new-normal lives. Our path began with a desire to help businesses connect with customers in an easy, affordable, and environmentally sustainable manner. We are backed by an adept crew who are always passionate about Blockchain and cryptocurrency. It is this passion that has made us stand here with a strong determination to make people adapt to cryptocurrencies.
The operation of MALUM is further strengthened by our native utility coin, MALUM COIN. It is a BEP-20 standard token that is built on the robust Binance Smart Chain network. The platform will make the best use of the network, including higher throughput rate, ultra-fast transactions, lower gas fees, etc.
